Beast Generator Damage Calculator

This page is for, surprisingly, generating Beasts! Beasts have several aspects that contribute to their stats: Genetic and Effort scores, plus the species base scores. If you've played pokemon, you might recognize these as IVs and EVs. These are similar, but not identical.

Beasts also have abilities that also resemble pokemon moves, though these are more restrictive. A standard attack, two "soulstrike" moves, and then magic abilities. In the game, these could be hypothetically infinite. Here? We're limiting it to 3 for simplicity.

Have fun generating your beasts! :)
