What is Beast Signer?

Beast Signer is an old Flash game from 2008 which only ever had a demo release. It was developed by Pseudolonewolf (NOT ME), creator of Mardek and Sindrel Song. In 2013, the game was effectively deemed 'abandoned' in favor of other projects, never to be updated again.

The Game's Premise

The premise of Beast Signer is fairly simple on the surface. There's a virtual reality called "The Machine", where many people are living following a nuclear war that wiped out most of the planet. The problem is, there are glitches in The Machine that manifest as monsters, termed 'Beasts'. In order to keep these beasts from terrorizing the people living in The Machine, you take on a position as a member of the Signer Elite! The mechanics are similar to Pokemon, with some key differences.
