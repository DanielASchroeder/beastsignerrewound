Beast Generator Damage Calculator

The formula for calculating damage is a little bit wonky. Sure, there's the normal elemental tic-tac-toe rock-paper-scissors that you would expect from a pokemon-like. What's more interesting is that the player's type impacts the damage dealt! An attack with the same type as the player's type gets a whole 10% boost to damage. Isn't that crazy?

Anyway, you'll only find standard damages here- many attacks have the capability to CRIT, dealing double damage! So keep that in mind.
